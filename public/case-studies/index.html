Our Portfolio

Proven Track Record of Excellence

Discover how Adventure Northern Escapes revitalises properties to create exceptional living spaces. Below are some of our most exciting projects and how they have made a difference.

XX Charnwood Street, St Helens

Project Process

1

Project Overview

Adventure Northern Escapes acquired XX Charnwood Street with the vision of transforming a dated and underutilised property into a modern, welcoming home for families.

2

Challenges

The property required significant refurbishment, including structural improvements, updating aged utilities, and enhancing its overall aesthetic appeal.

3

Solution

We partnered with Craft Joinery & Building Limited, led by Rob Craft, to carry out the refurbishment. Their expertise helped bring our vision to life, with modern finishes, improved energy efficiency, and a layout optimised for comfort and functionality.

4

Result

The renovated property has attracted quality tenants, and its value has significantly increased. It now serves as a comfortable and stylish rental property, helping contribute to the local community’s housing needs.

The Celestial Cabin (N29), Knaresborough

Project Process

1

Project Overview

The Celestial Cabin is a holiday home designed to offer an idyllic retreat in the beautiful surroundings of Knaresborough. Our aim was to create a memorable experience for holidaymakers seeking both comfort and nature.

2

Challenges

We wanted to blend the comfort of modern amenities with the rustic charm of a cabin environment, all while maximising the space available in the static caravan.

3

Solution

We upgraded the interiors with high-quality fittings, added efficient heating solutions, and worked on creating a light and airy atmosphere. Thoughtful design touches, such as a cosy outdoor seating area, ensured guests could make the most of the beautiful natural surroundings.

4

Result

The Celestial Cabin has become a popular getaway spot, with guests praising its comfort, style, and proximity to local attractions. It has delivered an outstanding return on investment, with high occupancy rates and excellent reviews from guests.

XX Creswell Street, St Helens

Project Process

1

Project Overview

XX Creswell Street required an extensive refurbishment to bring it back to life. We recognised the potential of this property to become a desirable residence for local families.

2

Challenges

The property had suffered from years of neglect and required a complete transformation—from wiring and plumbing upgrades to reconfiguration of interior spaces.

3

Solution

Our dedicated team worked closely with local builders and architects to maximise the available space and ensure the home was fully updated to modern standards. We focused on enhancing natural light, optimising the layout, and adding modern conveniences.

4

Result

XX Creswell Street is now a highly desirable rental property, boasting a bright and modern interior, energy-efficient fixtures, and increased market value. The refurbishment has breathed new life into the property, making it a welcoming home for tenants and a strong investment asset.
